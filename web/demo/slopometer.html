Now: your own prose

Paste anything — your last LinkedIn post, an email, a chatbot answer. Matches highlight as you type. Nothing leaves your browser.

This is the regex layer from the study — a deliberately narrow, auditable detector of one construction family (plus its affirmative cousin, counted separately, because the model reroutes into it when you silence the family). It is not an AI-text detector. Plenty of fine human writing uses antithesis; the AI tell is the rate, not the move. Every validation and false-positive audit is in the repo.
